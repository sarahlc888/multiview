# Task: Expand MET Museum criteria ✓

## Goal
Add new criteria to the MET Museum docset covering influence, expression, form, content, and animacy — creating richer multimodal evaluation dimensions.

## Type
ADD_CRITERIA

## Completed
Added 8 new criteria to `configs/available_criteria.yaml` under `met_museum:`:
- **animacy** — How animate/alive the artwork feels
- **expression** — Emotional expression in portraits and figurative works
- **form** — Physical form, medium, and material nature
- **content** — Conceptual/narrative meaning beyond literal subject matter
- **influence_network** — Stylistic lineage and shared artistic DNA
- **unusual_features** — What makes the artwork distinctive or stand out
- **number_of_people** — Count of human figures depicted
- **use_of_color** — Color palette choices and usage strategies
